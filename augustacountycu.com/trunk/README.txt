Drupal Incarnate Profile

===============================================

Client: Augusta County Federal Credit Union

Overview:  A local credit union serving the financial 
needs of residents of Augusta County, Virginia.

Primary Requirements:

	- 	informational site for interest rates, organizational 
		structure, promotions, and holiday closings, and
		annual financial reports
		
	- 	must be able to manage frequently changing information 
		without the need of a programmer or consultant
		
	- 	keep costs down for server management
	
Secondary Requirements:

	- 	some branding to relfect the identity of the organization
	
	- 	if mistakes are made on web, easily revert to previous 
		days content
